Data Methods

All authors numbers are based on their naming the species without another author, except in the case of Wood & Bright where the opposite is true, the figure only contains species they named together.

Species dates were cross-referenced with the author’s known birth and death, supposing that the author would be at least 18 when they began naming species, and allowing 2 years for any works to publsih post-humously. Certain authors were also cross-referenced with their known specialty (i.e. Coleoptera).

Due to the small number of unknown years for almost all authors besides three with unique names, these species were included with the author because otherwise these authors would not make the list and the name is unique enough to suppose these species belong to him. Common names like Smith therefore might be +/- 40 species.
